About Me

I am a graduate of the Bachelor's program at the Faculty of Architecture of Gdańsk University of Technology. I completed my studies with distinction, receiving the Gold Graduate Badge of Gdańsk University of Technology. During my studies, I became particularly interested in the topic of sustainable development in architecture. In my projects, I strive to focus on solutions that address these issues. Additionally, the use of AI in design is a subject I find extremely intriguing, as I believe it offers tremendous potential for enhancing the design process. I would like to pursue development in this field in the future.
